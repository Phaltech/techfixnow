About Tech Fix Now

Tech Fix Now Company Limited is dedicated to providing high-quality repair services for electronic devices. Our team consists of experienced technicians with expertise in various areas of specialization. We have established a presence in Sierra Leone and aim to expand our operations across Africa.

Our services include hardware and software repairs for mobile phones, tablets, computers, and gaming gadgets. Additionally, we offer accessories such as screens, protectors, chargers, and SD cards.

Market Analysis

The demand for reliable and professional technicians to repair electronic gadgets is significant in Africa. However, access to such services is limited in many regions. Tech Fix Now aims to address this gap by providing efficient and affordable repair solutions. With our mobile repair unit, we can reach customers in different communities and provide on-the-spot repairs. Our goal is to establish ourselves as the go-to repair company for tech gadgets in Africa.
